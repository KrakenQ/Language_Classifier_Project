Karolinów [karɔˈlinuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Tomaszów Mazowiecki, within Tomaszów Mazowiecki County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south of Tomaszów Mazowiecki and 53 km (33 mi) south-east of the regional capital Łódź.
