Krywe [ˈkrɨvɛ] (Ukrainian: Криве, Kryve) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lutowiska, within Bieszczady County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland, close to the border with Ukraine. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) west of Lutowiska, 22 km (14 mi) south of Ustrzyki Dolne, and 95 km (59 mi) south-east of the regional capital Rzeszów.The village has a population of 2.
