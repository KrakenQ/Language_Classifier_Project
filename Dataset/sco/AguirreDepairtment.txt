Aguirre Depairtment is a depairtment o Santiago del Estero Province, Argentinae. The caipital lees at Pinto.
The depairtment covers a aurie o 3692 km². The population as o 2010 wis 7,610. Municipalities include Argentina, Casares an Malbrán.
