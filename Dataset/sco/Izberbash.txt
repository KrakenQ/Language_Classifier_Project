Izberbash (Roushie: Избербаш) is a toun in the Republic o Dagestan, Roushie, locatit on the coast o the Caspian Sea 56 kilometer (35 mi) sootheast o Makhachkala. Population: 55,646 (2010 Census); 39,365 (2002 Census); 28,122 (1989 Census).


== History ==
It wis foondit in 1932 as an ile extraction dounset. Toun status wis grantit tae it in 1949.


== Admeenistrative an municipal status ==
Athin the framewirk o admeenistrative diveesions, it is incorporatit as the Toun o Izberbash—an admeenistrative unit wi the status equal tae that o the destricts. As a municipal diveesion, the Toun o Izberbash is incorporatit as Izberbash Urban Okrug.
