Järva Coonty (Estonie: Järva maakond), or Järvamaa (German: Jerwen, Laitin: Jervia), is ane o 15 counties o Estonie. It is situatit in central pairt o the kintra an borders Lääne-Viru Coonty tae the east, Jõgeva Coonty tae the sooth-east, Viljandi Coonty tae the sooth, Pärnu Coonty tae the sooth-wast, Rapla Coonty tae the wast, an Harju Coonty tae the north. 36,130 fowk live in Järva Coonty – constitutin 2.7% o the total population in Estonie (as o Januar 2009).


== History ==
In the first centuries AD poleetical an admeenistrative subdiveesions began tae emerge. Twa lairger subdiveesions appeared: the pairish (kihelkond) an the coonty (maakond). The pairish consistit o several veelages. Nearly aw pairishes haed at least ane fortress. The defence o the local aurie wis directit bi the heichest offeecial, the pairish elder. The coonty wis componit o several pairishes, heidit bi an elder an aw. Bi the 13t century the follaein major destricts haed developed in Estonie: Saaremaa (Osilia), Läänemaa (Rotalia or Maritima), Harjumaa (Harria), Rävala (Revalia), Virumaa (Vironia), Järvamaa (Jervia), Sakala (Saccala), an Ugandi (Ugaunia).


== Coonty Govrenment ==
The Coonty Govrenment (Estonie: Maavalitsus) is led bi Governor (Estonian: maavanem), who is appointit bi the Govrenment o Estonie for a term o five years. Syne 2009, the Govrenor position is held bi Tiina Oraste.
