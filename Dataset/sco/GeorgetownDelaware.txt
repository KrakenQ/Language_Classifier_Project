Georgetown is a toun in an the coonty seat o Sussex Coonty, Delaware, Unitit States. Accordin tae the 2010 census, the population o the toun is 6,422, an increase o 38.3% ower the previous decade.Georgetown is pairt o the Salisbury, Maryland-Delaware Metropolitan Statistical Area.
