Airchduke Karl Ambrosius o Austrick-Este (Karl Ambrosius Joseph Johann Baptist; 2 November 1785 â€“  2 September 1809) wis an Airchbishop o Esztergom. He wis a Prince o Modena bi virtue o his mither who wis the heiress tae the Duchy o Modena. He niver mairit an haed nae childer. He wis a brither o Queen Maria Theresa o Sardinie an Electress Maria Leopoldine o Bavarie an Duke Francis IV o Modena. He wis a nephew o Marie Antoinette an they shared a birthday.
