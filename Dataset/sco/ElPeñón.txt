El Peñón (Spainyie: the rock) mey refer tae:

El Peñón, Barahona, a toun in the Barahona province o the Dominican Republic.
El Peñón, Catamarca, a veelage an municipality in Catamarca Province in northwastren Argentinae.
El Peñón, Bolívar, a toun an municipality locatit in the Bolívar Depairtment, northren Colombie.
El Peñón, Cundinamarca, a toun an municipality o Colombie in the depairtment o Cundinamarca
El Peñón, Santander, a toun an municipality in the Santander Depairtment in northeastren Colombie.
El Peñón (Antarcticae), a monolithic formation on Livingston Island in the Sooth Shetland Islands, Antarcticae.
