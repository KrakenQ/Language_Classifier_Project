Ilulissat, formerly Jakobshavn, is a toun in the Qaasuitsup municipality in wastren Greenland, locatit approximately 200 km (120 mi) north o the Arctic Circle. Wi the population o 4,541 as o 2013, it is the third-lairgest dounset in Greenland, efter Nuuk an Sisimiut.
