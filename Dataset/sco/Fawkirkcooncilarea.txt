Fawkirk Cooncil Area (Inglis: Falkirk Council Area, Scots Gaelic: Comhairle na h-Eaglaise Brice) is ane o the 32 cooncil areas o Scotland. It haes mairches wi North Lanrikshire til the soothwast, Stirlin til the nor'wast, Wast Lowden til the sootheist an, athort the Firth o Forth til the nor'eist, Fife an Clackmannanshire. The cooncil area wis shapit on 1 Aprile 1996 frae the same mairches o Fawkirk Destrik Cooncil bi wey o the Local Govrenment etc. (Scotland) Act 1994. Afore 1975 maist o the cooncil area wis pairt o the coontie o Stirlinshire an a wee pairt, namelie Bo'ness an Blackness, wis pairt o the umwhyl coontie o Wast Lowden.
