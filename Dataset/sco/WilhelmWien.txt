Wilhelm Carl Werner Otto Fritz Franz Wien (German: [ˈviːn]; 13 Januar 1864 – 30 August 1928) wis a German pheesicist wha, in 1893, uised theories aboot heat an electromagnetism tae deduce Wien's displacement law, which calculates the emission o a blackbouk at ony temperatur frae the emission at ony ane reference temperatur.
