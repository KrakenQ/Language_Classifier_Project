The Province o Imperia (It. Provincia di Imperia) is a muntainous an hilly province, in the Ligurie region o Italy, situatit atween the Maritime Alps tae the north an the Ligurie Sea, an airm o the Mediterranean Sea tae the sooth. Its caipital is the ceety o Imperia.
