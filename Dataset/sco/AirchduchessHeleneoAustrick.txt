Airchduchess Helene o Austrick (Helene Marie Alice Christine Josefa Anna Margareta Madeleine Walburga Blandina Cäcilie Philomena Carmela Ignatia Rita de Cascia; 30 October 1903 – 8 September 1924) wis a member o the Tuscan branch o the Hoose o Habsburg-Lorraine an an Airchduchess o Austrick an Princess o Bohemie, Hungary, an Tuscany bi birth. Through her mairiage tae Philipp Albrecht, Hereditary Duke o Württemberg, Helene wis also a member o the Hoose o Württemberg and Hereditary Duchess  o Württemberg


== Mairiage ==
Helene mairit Philipp Albrecht, Hereditary Duke o Württemberg, auldest child an son o Albrecht, Duke o Württemberg an his wife, Airchduchess Margarete Sophie o Austrick, on 24 October 1923 in Altshausen. Helene an Philipp Albrecht haed ane dochter.


=== Issue ===
Duchess Maria Christina o Württemberg (born 2 September 1924), mairit Prince Georg Hartmann o Liechtenstein, son o Prince Aloys o Liechtenstein.
