Berlare is a municipality locatit in the Belgian province o East Flanders. The municipality comprises the touns o Berlare proper, Overmere an Uitbergen. On 1 Januar 2006 Berlare haed a total population o 14,092. The total aurie is 37.82 km² whilk gies a population densitie o 373 indwallers per km². The current mayor o Berlare is Karel De Gucht, frae the Leeberal pairty VLD, an aa European Commissioner for Trade. Donkmeer, a muckle loch, is locatit here.
