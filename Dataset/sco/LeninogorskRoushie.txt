Leninogorsk (Roushie: Лениного́рск; Tatar Cyrillic: Лениногорск, Laitin: Leninogorsk) is a toun in Tatarstan, Roushie, located 322 kilometer (200 mi) sootheast o Kazan. Population: 64,127 (2010 Census); 65,592 (2002 Census); 62,093 (1989 Census).


== Admeenistrative an municipal status ==
Athin the framewirk o admeenistrative diveesions, Leninogorsk serves as the administrative centre o Leninogorsky Destrict, even tho it is no a pairt o it. As a admeenistrative diveesion, it is incorporatit separately as the toun o republic significance o Leninogorsk—an administrative unit wi the status equal tae that o the destricts. As a municipal diveesion, the toun o republic significance o Leninogorsk is incorporatit athin Leninogorsky Municipal Destrict as Leninogorsk Urban Dounset.
