Edgar (Middle Gaelic: Étgar mac Maíl Choluim; Modren Gaelic: Eagar mac Mhaoil Chaluim), kent bi the eik-name Probus ("the Wicht") an aa (c. 1074 – 8 Januar 1107), wis King o Scotland frae 1097 tae 1107.
