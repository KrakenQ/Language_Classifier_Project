2013 (MMXIII) is a common year stairtin on a Tysday.


== Events ==
Februar 28 – Benedict XVI resigns as pape, acomin the first tae dae sae syne Gregory XII in 1415, an the first tae dae sae voluntarily syne Celestine V in 1294.
Mairch 13 – Cardinal Jorge Mario Bergoglio o Argentinae is electit the 266t pape, whereupon he taks the name Francis an acomes the first Jesuit pape, the first pape frae the Americas, an the first pape frae the Soothren Hemisphere.
