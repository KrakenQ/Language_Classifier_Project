Philip o Naples an Sicily, "Duke o Calabrie", Infante o Spain (Filippo Antonio Gennaro Pasquale Francesco de Paula; 13 Juin 1747 – 19 September 1777) wis the son an heir o Carlo I o Sicilie, Carlo VII o Naples (future King Carlos III o Spain. Born in Portici, ootside Naples he wis gien the teetle Duke o Calabrie (title for the heir tae the throne o Naples) at birth. He never became king due tae the disabilities he haed sae the throne went tae his younger brither Infant Carlos. He wis named in honour o his grandfaither Keeng Philip V o Spain.
