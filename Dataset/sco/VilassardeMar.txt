Vilassar de Mar (Catalan pronunciation: [biɫəˈsa ðə ˈmar]) is a municipality in the comarca o the Maresme in Catalonie, Spain. It is situatit on the coast atween Premià de Mar an Cabrera de Mar, tae the north-east o Barcelona. The toun is baith a tourist centre an a dormitory toun for Barcelona, an is kent for its horticultur an aw. The main N-II road an a RENFE railwey line run throu the toun, while a local road connects the municipality wi the C-32 autopista at Vilassar de Dalt an Mataró an Barcelona. The famous canned fuid company DANI is based in Vilassar de Mar comin frae a Vilassar faimily.
