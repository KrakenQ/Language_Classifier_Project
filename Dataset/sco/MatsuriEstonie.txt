Matsuri (kent as Matsurdi, Matšuri, Belohvostovo an Piiroja an aw) is a veelage in Värska Pairish, Põlva Coonty, in sootheastren Estonie, on the mairch wi Roushie. The Tartu–Pechory, Valga–Pechory railweys an Karisilla–Pechory road (nr. 63) aw pass Matsuri an cross the mairch in neebourin Koidula. Syne 2008 a new Koidula railwey station is bein biggit on the territory o Matsuri tae enable the traffec atween Tartu–Pechory an Valga–Pechory tae avyde crossin the Roushie border.
Matsuri haes a population o 42 (as o 1 Januar 2011).In 2008 a new Matsuri–Sesniki road wis opened, makkin it possible tae reach Saatse an its neebourin veelages athoot necessarily passin throu the Saatse Buit.
