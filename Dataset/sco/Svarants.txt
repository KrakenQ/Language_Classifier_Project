Svarants (Armenie: Սվարանց, Romanisit as Svarants’ an aw) is a veelage an rural commonty (municipality)  in the Syunik Province o Armenia.  The Naitional Statistical Service o the Republic o Armenie (ARMSTAT) reportit its population wis 270 in 2010, doun frae 360 at the 2001 census.
