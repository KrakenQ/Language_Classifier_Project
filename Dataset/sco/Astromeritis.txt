Astromeritis (Greek: Αστρομερίτης) is a lairge veelage in the Morphou Depairtment o the Nicosia Destrict o Cyprus. It is locatit 30 km (18.64 mi) frae the caipital Nicosia. Bi the 2001 census it haed a total population o 2,361.


== Green Line crossing ==
Astromeritis is the location o ane o sax crossins athort the UN Buffer Zone atween the aurie effectively controlled bi the Republic o Cyprus an the unrecognisit 'Turkis Republic o Northren Cyprus'. The toun on the northren side o the border is Zodeia (Turkis: Bostancı). The crossin is anerlie for vehicular traffic.
The crossin wis opened on 31 August 2005.
