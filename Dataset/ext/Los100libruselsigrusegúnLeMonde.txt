Los 100 librus el sigru es una crassificacion de los librus consideraus como los cien mejoris del sigru 20, redatau ena primavera de 1999 por mé duna encuesta realizá pola cadena francesa Fnac i el periódicu Le Monde de París.
(ingrés)——>
Starting from a preliminary list of 200 titles created by bookshops and journalists, 17,000 French voted by responding to the question, "Which books have stayed in your memory?" (« Quels livres sont restés dans votre mémoire ? »).The list of acclaimed titles mixes great novels with poetry and theatre, as well as the comic strip. The first fifty works on the list were the subject of an essay by Frédéric Beigbeder, The Last Inventory Before Liquidation, in which he notably drew attention to its French-centred character.
