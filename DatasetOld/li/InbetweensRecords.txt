Inbetweens Records is saer 1998 e Limburgs CD-label dat opereert vanoet Elsloo. 't Eenmansbedrief is in heng van Jos Starmans dae zich väör d'r naam leet inspirere dör e fragmaent oet 'ne song van The Only Ones: Caught between right or wrong, tell me there's no escaping?
Bekaande Limburgse muzikaante en zengers die bie Inbetween Records hön CD's oetbrachte zeunt o.m.:

Mike Roelofs (pianist)
Mo'Jones (Ingelsjtalig)
