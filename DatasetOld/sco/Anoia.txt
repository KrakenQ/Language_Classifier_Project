Anoia (Catalan pronunciation: [əˈnɔjə]) is a comarca (coonty) in central Catalonie, Spain, wi its caipital at Igualada.
The comarca o l'Anoia is irrigatit bi the Anoia River; the leadin industry is the makkin o paper.
Tae the north are Solsonès an Bages, tae the wast, Baix Llobregat tae the sooth Baix Penedès an Alt Camp, an tae the east Conca de Barberà an Segarra.
