San Pedro Topiltepec is a toun an municipality in Oaxaca in sooth-wastren Mexico. It is pairt o the Teposcolula Destrict in the centre o the Mixteca Region. The name "Topiltepec" means "The Sheriff Hill".The municipality kivers a aurie o 45.93 kmÂ² at a altitude o 2,170 metre abuin sea leivel. The climate is mild wi nae mony variation throu the year. Flora includes roses, geraniums, lilies, regina, bougainvillea an jasmine flouers, an aik, pine, juniper, ramonal, ash an arbutus trees.
As o 2005, the municipality haed 109 hoosehaulds wi a total population o 372 twa o wham spak a indigenous leid.
Maist o the fowk are devotit tae agricultur, plantin o corn, beans an wheat.
A smaw proportion o the population is engagit in feshin up beef nowt an pigs.
