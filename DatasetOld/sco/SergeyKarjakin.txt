Sergey Alexandrovich Karjakin ([Сергій Олександрович Карякін, Serhiy Oleksandrovych Karjakin] ; Roushie: Серге́й Алекса́ндрович Каря́кин; born Januar 12, 1990) is a Ukrainian-born Roushie chess grandmaster. He wis a chess prodigy an haulds the record for the warld's youngest Grandmaster, at the age o 12 years an 7 month. Karjakin reached the feenal o the Warld Chess Championship in 2016, but lost tae Magnus Carlsen.
