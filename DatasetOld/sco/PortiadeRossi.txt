Portia Lee James DeGeneres (born Amanda Lee Rogers; 31 Januar 1973), kent professionally as Portia de Rossi /ˈpɔərʃə də ˈrɒsi/, is an Australie-American actress, model an philanthropist, kent for her roles as lawyer Nelle Porter on the telly series Ally McBeal an Lindsay Fünke on the sitcom Arrested Development.
