Ussuriysk (Roushie: Уссури́йск) is a ceety in Primorsky Krai, Roushie, locatit in the fertile valley o the Razdolnaya River, 98 kilometer (61 mi) north o Vladivostok an aboot 60 kilometer (37 mi) frae baith the Cheenese border an the Paceefic Ocean. Population:  157,759 (2002 Census); 158,016 (1989 Census).
