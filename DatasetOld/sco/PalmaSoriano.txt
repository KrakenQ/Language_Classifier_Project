Palma Soriano is a municipality in the Santiago de Cuba Province o Cuba.
The municipality is dividit intae the localite o Alto Cedro, Caney del Sitio, Guaninao, José Martí, Juan Barón, La Concepción, Las Cuchillas, Los Dorados, Norte, Palmarito de Cauto, San Leandro, San Ramón, Santa Filomena an Sur.The ceety o Palma Soriano is locatit on the banks o the upper Cauto River. The ceety wis foondit in 1825, unitin ranches (haciendas) that stuid in this aurie, an wis the place whaur the body o José Martí (the leader o the Cuban unthirldom muivement, killed in 1895, in the Battle o Dos Ríos.) wis brocht afore it was laid in its feenal restin place in Santiago de Cuba.
