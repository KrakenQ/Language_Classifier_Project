f1Mount Ware is a 1.533 m hoha Beag im ostantarktischn Mac-Robertson-Land. In da Porthos Range vo de Prince Charles Mountains rogda unmiddlboa sidli van Mount Kerr af.
Luftafnohma vo da Australian National Antarctic Research Expeditions aus'm Joar 1965 hom seina Koartiarung diand. As Antarctic Names Committee of Australia hod eahm noch William R. Ware bnennd, Wedabobochta af da Mawson-Station im Joar 1968.
