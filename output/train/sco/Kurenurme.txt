Kurenurme (Võro: Kurõnurmõ) is a veelage in Sõmerpalu Pairish, Võru Coonty, in sootheastren Estonie. As o 2000 it haed a population o 73.Kurenurme wis foondit as a lumberjack veelage in the beginnin o the 19t century.Kurenurme kool wis foondit in 1931, it wis closed in 2001.Kurenurme haes a station on currently inactive Valga–Pechory railwey.
