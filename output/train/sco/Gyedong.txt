Gye-dong is a dong, neebourheid o Jongno-gu in Seoul, Sooth Korea. It is a legal dong (법정동 法定洞) admeenistered unner its admeenistrative dong (행정동 行政洞), Gahoe-dong.The aurie is hame tae Joongang Bath, Korea's auldest public bathhoose, situatit on the northren end o the main street. It haes a auld hardware store an sesame ile shop frae the 1970s an aw, as well as eateries an cafes in the same style.
