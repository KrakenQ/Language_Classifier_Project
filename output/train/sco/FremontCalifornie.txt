Fremont (/ˈfriːmɒnt/ free-mont) is a ceety in Alameda County, Californie, Unitit States. It wis incorporatit on Januar 23, 1956, frae the merger o five smawer communities: Centerville, Niles, Irvington, Mission San Jose, an Warm Springs. The ceety is named efter American splorer John Charles Frémont.
