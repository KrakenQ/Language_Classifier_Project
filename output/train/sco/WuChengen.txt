Wu Cheng'en (traditeeonal Cheenese: 吳承恩; simplifeed Cheenese: 吴承恩; pinyin: Wú​ Chéng​'ēn, c. 1500–1582 or 1505–1580), coortesy name Ruzhong (汝忠), wis a Cheenese novelist an poet o the Ming Dynasty, an is conseedered bi mony tae be the author o Journey tae the Wast, ane o the Fower Great Clessical Novelles o Cheenese leeteratur.
