Lebanon (pronounced /ˈlɛbənɒn/ (deprecatit template) or /ˈlɛbənən/; Arabic: لُبْنَان‎ Lubnān; French: Liban), offeecially the Republic o Lebanon (Arabic: اَلْجُمْهُورِيَّة اَللُّبْنَانِيَّة al-Jumhūrīyah al-Lubnānīyah; French: République libanaise), is a kintra on the eastren shore o the Mediterranean Sea. It is bordered bi Sirie tae the north an east, an Israel tae the sooth. Lebanon's location at the crossroads o the Mediterranean Basin an the Arabian hinterland haes dictatit its rich history, an shapit a cultural identity o releegious an ethnic diversity.
