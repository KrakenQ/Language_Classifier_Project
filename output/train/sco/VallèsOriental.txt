Vallès Oriental [bəˈʎɛz uɾjənˈtaɫ] is a comarca (coonty) in Catalonie, Spain.  Its caipital is Granollers. Alang wi Vallès Occidental it forms the grand comarca o Vallès.
In Mey 2015, Vallès Oriental lost fower municipalities - Castellcir, Castellterçol, Granera, Sant Quirze Safaja - tae the new comarca o Moianès.
