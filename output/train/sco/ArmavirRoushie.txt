Armavir (Roushie: Армави́р) is a ceety in Krasnodar Krai, Roushie, locatit on the left bank o the Kuban River. Population: 188,832 (2010 Census); 193,964 (2002 Census); 160,983 (1989 Census); 144,000 (1969). Armavir wis umwhile the seicont-lairgest industrial centre o Krasnodar Krai, efter Krasnodar.
