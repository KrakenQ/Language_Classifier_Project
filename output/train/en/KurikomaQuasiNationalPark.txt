Kurikoma Quasi-National Park (栗駒国定公園, Kurikoma Kokutei Kōen) is a Quasi-National Park that extends between Akita, Iwate, Miyagi, and Yamagata Prefectures, Japan. 
Established in 1968, the central feature of the park is Mount Kurikoma (栗駒山) at 1,627 m (5,338 ft).
It is rated a protected landscape (category II) according to the IUCN.Like all Quasi-National Parks in Japan, the park is managed by the local prefectural governments.
