Sitee is a 1993 Maldivian drama film written and directed by Ahmed Nimal. Produced by Aslam Rasheed under Slam Studio in association with Kid Productions, the film stars Nimal and Fathimath Rameeza in pivotal roles.The film is based on true incidents that occurred to a friend of Ahmed Nimal. Filming was completed in one month while shooting for the songs was extended for another month. The song "Mulhi Jaan Hithaa" is shot in five different islands from five atolls.
